Hello, survivors! Lace up your hiking boots and prepare yourself for the cutest apocalypse of all time. With your help, Bub can survive and even learn to thrive in this dangerous and adorable world.


Setting off with little more than the clothes on his back, Bub must navigate a hostile wilderness teeming with zombies, wildlife, resources, and mysteries. If the prospect of zombie hordes doesn't frighten you then you might even find yourself wandering the abandoned towns of old-America to plunder the riches and secrets that remain.

Explore an infinite, procedurally generated world to gather what you need, brave the elements, build a sustainable home base, and make some friends along the way.

Gather, farm, fish, craft, build, trap, fortify, explore, and loot your way to post-apocalyptic domination.


Become self-sustaining with smokers and wells or grow the prettiest flower garden this side of Halcyon. What kind of base do you want to call home?

Traps in Wanderlost have a variety of purposes. A Deadfall trap can be great for guarding your walls but must be reset after each use. Springboard traps might not deal any damage on their own but they can fling zombies backward allowing you to use other traps like spike pits and barbed wires very effectively. Smaller traps like the snare and figure-4 trap may not be big enough to capture zombies but they do a fine job capturing small game. Just be sure to set your traps somewhere you know animals might wander by.



When the berries rot and the animals hibernate, you'll need to have a good stock of food preserved. When temperatures plummet at the height of winter, stay near your fire or bundle yourself up in furs to survive. And while water can be easy to come by in the wilderness, it's rarely clean. Be sure to filter or boil it first!


Finding food in the wild can be difficult, so why not create a reliable source instead? You won’t be starting out with a pocket full of turnip seeds, but farming can provide an abundance of food in the late-game when you're able to invest your time. And different biomes are suited to different crops so choose your farm location carefully.

The waters are teeming with life, but how do you reach it? Craft a simple fishing pole to catch a bite close to shore. For deeper waters, you will need different tools. Or you could try wading into the water yourself, just be careful you don’t scare the fish away or get too cold with no way to dry off.

Track your prey or lure them to you. All animals in Wanderlost leave footprints behind which can give you a decent picture of the local wildlife if you can read the ground. Hunting & tracking an animal isn't easy, they're fast and you could be gone for a day or more. Being adequately prepared, with a leather waterskin and some dried meats, can make the difference between a successful hunt and a failed one. Animals provide valuable food, skins, and other crafting supplies like bones and animal fat. Stalk your prey using a bow or rifle, or you can let your ingenuity do the work by setting traps.


An encounter with zombies can be deadly. Making sure you have the right tools and weapons to suit your playstyle is key. Each weapon has advantages and disadvantages so lining up your character's strengths with your weapon's strengths can make you a deadly force in post-apocalyptic America.


Bub can't survive on good looks alone. Here are some of the items, tools, and weapons that help Bub live to see another day:

  •  Spears let you maintain distance, but can be fragile
  •  Blunt weapons can stun zombies and sometimes even knock them back
  •  Bladed weapons slice through your enemies, striking more than one target
  •  Bows are stealthy and powerful, but can be slower to use 
  •  Guns are unparalleled in destructive force, however, the noise will draw attention and ammo isn't cheap

Common resources like wood and stone are highly useful, but for more advanced crafting recipes you may have to scour the world for rare biomes that harbor crucial materials. For example, you can find peat and bog-iron in the bog. Diverse world biomes motivate exploration and can affect what makes the most successful base location.


Protect yourself from the elements and add an extra layer of safety between you and the attacks of the powerful undead. Equipment in Wanderlost has 4 main stats, Armor, Insulation, Temperature, and Rain Protection. Use the hides and resources that you collect to craft clothing and armor capable of keeping you dry, warm, and safe from attacks.


It's important to make use of the downtime in between hunting and looting expeditions. Use this precious time to craft game arrows, weave cordage for infrastructure, make flesh hides for tanning, reload your weapons, and more - preparation is key to survival!



As big as the world is, you are certainly not alone. Animals in Wanderlost range from the common squirrel or rabbit in temperate biomes to the exotic, deadly panther of the rainforest or alligator of the swamp.

Zombies in Wanderlost differ in more than appearance. Each zombie type has unique behaviors that significantly change the tactics and strategy of combat.

  •  Basic Zombies are stock-standard. Possessing average speed and capable of only close-ranged attacks, they might not seem threatening... until you're surrounded
  •  Acid Zombies are mercifully rare as they tend to linger in the Swamp biome and radioactive POIs. Their acid spit is fast, lethal, and long-ranged. Even if you avoid the spit, you'll have to contend with the puddle of acid left behind
  •  Chubby Zombies seem harmless at first glance but what they lack in speed they make up for in range and power. By slamming the ground with their fists, these zombies can send out a devastating shockwave
  •  Grapple Zombies are one of the least dangerous, yet most dreaded zombies you'll encounter. They may deal minimal damage but they'll jump on your back, leaving you slow and encumbered just long enough for their Zombie friends to close in

Exploration involves great risk because the abandoned structures, towns, and cities of old America have the highest zombie density. Despite the risks, it may hold the most valuable loot in the game. After all, guns don't grow on trees.


While fighting zombies is necessary for survival, Wanderlost also has deep RPG mechanics. Each level-up yields primary attributes such as strength, intelligence, constitution, and dexterity. The way you build Bub determines his strengths and weaknesses. 

Are you an assassin who strikes quick and fatal blows before escaping into the darkness? Or perhaps a rifle-toting gunslinger capable of taking on an entire horde? You decide!


Halcyon is one of the few survivor holdouts in post-apocalyptic America. You're likely to make some friends here as well as meet some important shopkeepers and quest-givers. Living, breathing humans are a bit hard to come by in the post-apocalypse so best to cherish the ones you've got.
